#+SETUPFILE: config.org
#+TITLE: cuban pork stew

* ingredients
** a
   - 1 lb pork (bite-size)
   - 2 tsp olive oil
   - pinch of salt
** b
   - 1/2 onion chopped
   - 14.5 oz can chopped tomatoes
   - 1/2 bunch fresh cilantro coarsely chopped
   - 1/3 cup minced garlic
   - 1 1/2 tbsp fresh lime juice
   - 2 tbsp brown sugar
   - 1 tsp cumin
** c
   - 14 oz can black beans
* instructions
  1. heat olive oil in stew pan and brown meat
  2. add salt and onion, cook till lightly browned
  3. add [[b]]
  4. cover and simmer for 1 hour, stirring occasionally
  5. add beans and simmer for 5 minutes
  6. serve over cooked rice with goat cheese
