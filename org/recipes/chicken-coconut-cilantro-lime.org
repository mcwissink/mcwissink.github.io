#+SETUPFILE: config.org
#+TITLE: chicken coconut cilantro lime sauce

* ingredients
** a
   - 1 1/2 pounds boneless chicken breasts
   - 2 tbsp olive oil
   - 2 tbsp butter
   - 2 green onions (green and white separated)
   - 2 cloves garlic (minced)
   - 1 can unsweetened coconut milk
   - 2-3 tbsp fresh chopped cilantro
   - kosher salt and fresh black pepper
** b
   - 1/2 cup chicken stock
   - 2 tbsp fresh lime juice
   - zest of 1 lime
   - 2 tbsp honey
   - 1/2 tsp red pepper flakes
* instructions
  1. slice chicken in half lengthwise
  2. pound each cutlet
  3. season with salt and pepper
  4. melt butter with 1 tbsp oil in pan over medium heat
  5. add chicken when hot and sear until golden brown
  6. remove chicken from pan
  7. add 1 tbsp olive oilk, green onions (white), and garlic until fragrant
  8. add [[b]] and keep at a simmer for 3-4 minutes
  9. stir in coconut milk and season with salt and pepper
  10. simmer for 4-5 minutes
  11. return chicken to pan until chicken is cooked (4-5 minutes)
  12. stir in green onion (green) and cilantro
