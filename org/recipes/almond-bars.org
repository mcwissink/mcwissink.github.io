#+SETUPFILE: config.org
#+TITLE: almond bars

* ingredients
** a
   - 1 cup butter
   - 2 cup sugar
   - 4 eggs
   - 2 cup flour
   - 2 tsp almond extract
   - pinch of salt
** b
   - 1 tbsp sugar
   - 1/2 cup sliced almonds
* instructions
  1. mix [[a]]
  2. sprinkle with [[b]]
  3. bake at 350 for 30-40 min
