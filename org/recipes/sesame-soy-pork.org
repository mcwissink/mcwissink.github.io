#+SETUPFILE: config.org
#+TITLE: sesame soy pork

* ingredients
** a
   - 1.25 lbs ground pork
** b
   - 2 carrots (shredded)
   - 1/3 cup sesame dressing
   - 8 tbsp sweet soy glaze
** c 
   - 1 tbsp butter
   - 2 limes
** d
   - 1.5 tbsp sweet thai chili sauce
   - 2 tbsp mayonnaise
** e
   - 1 oz crispy fried onions
   - 0.5 oz cilantro (1/3 bunch) (chopped)
* instructions
** rice 
   1. cook rice
   2. zest limes
   3. stir [[c]] into rice
** chili mayo
   1. combine [[d]]
** pork
   1. heat pork in pan with salt and oil
   2. cook until brown
   3. add [[b]]
   4. cook until carrots tender and sauce thick
   5. season to taste with salt/pepper
